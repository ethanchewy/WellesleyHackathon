About Us

WellesleyHacks will bring together more than 300 high schoolers who have a passion for learning and innovating. These students would form groups, generate ideas, experiment with ideas, and finally deploy their idea as an application. This 10 hour event showcases inspiring speakers, engaging workshops, and prominent judges.

What We will do

We welcome any creative mind regardless of whether you are a hacker, maker, or designer.
You can make anything that pertains to one or more of these categories:

Mobile Apps
Web Apps
Hardware
