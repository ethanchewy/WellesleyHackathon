What is WellesleyHacks?




About Us

WellesleyHacks will bring together more than 300 high schoolers and middle schoolers who have a passion for learning and innovating. These students would form groups, generate ideas, experiment with ideas, and finally deploy their idea as an application. This 10 hour event showcases inspiring speakers, engaging workshops, and prominent judges.


What We will do

We welcome any creative mind regardless of whether you are a hacker, maker, or designer.
You can make anything that pertains to one or more of these categories:

Mobile Apps
Web Apps
Hardware

FAQ


Who can come to WellesleyHacks?

Any Highschooler. We welcome highschool hackers of all skill levels!

How are teams formed?

2 months before the hackathon, there will be a Facebook group created and you can find team members there. There will also be a breakfast in the morning and you can create teams there!

Will there be food?

Yes! Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a lot of yummy snacks.

What should I bring?

You should bring a laptop and wear comfortable clothing.

Is this Event Free?

Yes!

How many team members can I have?

4. You can also hack solo but hacking with a team is always more fun!

Travel Reimbursements?

As of now, there will be none.

What about Hardware?

We are trying to get some hardware. Most likely there will be raspberry pis provided.

Can I start working on my hack before the event?

No. If you are really itching to get started, feel free to familiarize yourself with the programming languages, hardward, and/or frameworks you plan to use.
