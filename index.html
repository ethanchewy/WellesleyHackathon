About Us

WellesleyHacks will bring together more than 400 high schoolers who have a passion for learning and innovating. These students would form groups, generate ideas, experiment with ideas, and finally deploy their idea as an application. This 10 hour event showcases inspiring speakers, engaging workshops, and prominent judges.
